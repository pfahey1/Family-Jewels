Instructions

Win Condition

The goal of Family Jewels is to reach a cash stash of $2000 in as little days as possible. What is best to keep in mind is buy low and sell high.

Current Statistics

This box shows you your current player statistics such as current economy day, player cash, and gem inventory.

Current Market Prices

This box shows you the current market prices for each gem for the current day. The prices have a 50% fluctuation for each gem when a new day occurs.

Options

This box shows you the options you have available to you. Can either choose new day to change the market prices, sell a specific jewel & specific amount, buy a specific jewel & specific amount, or reset the game to initial values.

Action Box

This box is where you enter the jewel & quantity you want to either buy or sell and buttons to trigger those actions or cancel them.
